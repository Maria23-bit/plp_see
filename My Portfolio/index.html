About Mary Khakame

Hello! I'm Mary Khakame, the passionate founder behind Creative Akili. My journey as a business consultant specialist is driven by a deep commitment to nurturing the vibrant entrepreneurial spirit within Kenya. I specialize in working with startups, helping aspiring founders transform raw ideas into robust, market-ready businesses.

My expertise lies in understanding the unique challenges and immense opportunities present in the Kenyan market. I guide entrepreneurs through crucial stages, from idea validation and strategic planning to building strong operational foundations and compelling brand identities.

I believe that with the right 'Akili' (intellect and insight) and tailored support, any innovative idea can flourish. My approach is hands-on, collaborative, and always focused on delivering actionable strategies that lead to tangible growth.
